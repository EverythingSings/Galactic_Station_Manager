In the year 3057, Humanity has spread across the stars. You are the newly appointed commander of GSM-42, a critical outpost in a resource-rich (but potentially dangerous) sector.

Your mission is to manage resources, advance research, and prepare for possible first contact with an unknown alien civilization.

Mission Details

Utilize your resources wisely to build and upgrade structures, ensuring a sustainable functioning of your station. This will allow us to generate passive resources.

Engage in research to unlock new technologies and improve efficiency. This is critical for our expansion across the stars.

Monitor your resources carefully, balancing extraction and expenditure to maximize your station's capabilities. Stay alert for mission updates and new opportunities.

Your decisions will shape the future of Humanity. Good luck, Humanity is in your hands!

About Galactic Station Manager

Welcome to Galactic Station Manager, an immersive space strategy game where you take on the role of a station commander in the far reaches of the galaxy.

Manage resources, conduct research, engage in diplomacy, and lead your station to prosperity in this ever-expanding universe.

How to Play

  • Collect and manage resources
  • Upgrade your station's facilities
  • Research new technologies
  • Complete missions
  • Engage in intergalactic diplomacy
